Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions before you reach out.

We appreciate bug reports! Please use the contact form above and select "Bug Report" as the subject. Include the tool name, what you were trying to do, what happened, and your browser/version if possible. Screenshots are always helpful.
